neon optics documentation ×

this simulation models physical ray optics, reflection, refraction, portals, and chromatic dispersion.

mouse controls:

  • left click & drag: reposition components on the optical breadboard.
  • rotation handle: drag the small circle extending from a selected component to rotate it.
  • selection: click any component to open its detailed inspector variables on the right.

optical behaviors:

  • laser emitter: casts light beams. white light is a compound beam that will disperse into a rainbow spectrum when refracted!
  • prisms / lenses: bends rays based on snell's law and their base refractive index. white light separates because different colors refract at slightly offset index parameters.
  • beam splitters: half-silvered mirrors that reflect 50% of the light energy and transmit 50%.
  • portals: teleports light beams across space while retaining relative entry angles and velocities.
  • targets: sensors that require alignment. some targets are wavelength-specific (they only activate when hit by their matching color!).
